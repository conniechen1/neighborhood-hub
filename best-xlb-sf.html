Dumpling Story

The clear #1 by volume and consistency. 77% of reviews are 5-star, which is exceptional for a dumpling spot. Known for a creative take — beef XLB pan fried, numb-and-spicy, lava custard — but the classic pork XLB holds up too. Cocktails round it out for a full evening.

HK Lounge Bistro
★★★★ 174 reviews Mission District Best dim sum spread

If you want XLB as part of a full dim sum experience, this is your spot. Reviewers consistently praise the XLB specifically: "juicy, strong skin, no metal tin" (a mark of XLB made in-house) and "rich in soup, thin skin." Best for groups who want variety alongside the dumplings.
"The XLB was juicy and the skin was so strong — you know it will be a good dumpling when there is no metal tin."
"The xiaolongbao is rich in soup, the skin is thin and the filling is fresh."

The most-reviewed spot in the data but more polarizing — 49% 5-star and 13% 1-star means strong opinions both ways. When it's good, the XLB is described as "the plumpest and tastiest in the city." Consistency issues are the recurring complaint. Worth trying — go on a weeknight.
