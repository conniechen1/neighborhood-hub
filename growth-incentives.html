SEO Sign-Up Driver
Target: High-intent SEO traffic who bounce without registering

"See real prices from your neighborhood ‚Äî free with a Yelp account"
The most common question before any home services job is "how much should this cost?" Gate a hyperlocal cost guide ‚Äî built from actual review-mentioned prices ‚Äî behind free account creation. Why it works
Yelp has the best local price data anywhere ‚Äî reviews mention actual dollar amounts at higher rates than any competitor
Cost-check intent correlates directly with booking intent: people pricing jobs are days away from hiring someone
Soft gate (free account) has far lower drop-off than paywalls ‚Äî captures 60‚Äì80% vs. 10‚Äì20% for paid
Unlocks retargeting: once the email is captured, follow up when they are ready to book
The data already exists in merged_reviews.csv ‚Äî this is a zero-cost feature to ship
